ECE-Department at O.U

Established in 1959, as the first department specializing in the field of Electronics and Communication, the Department of E.C.E., O.U.C.E., has scaled unprecedented heights in nurturing technical expertise and delivering quality professionals to the industry.

The Department was accredited by NBA with 'A' Grade for five years and is ISO 9001 certified. The APSC has graded this department with a score of 7.9. In recognition of their pioneering contributions to the fields of Electronics and Communication, three teachers received the best teacher state awards and a faculty member received "Vishveshwarayya" Award from IE and also an IETE award.

The Department has also contributed learning modules in the areas of Communications, Digital Signal Processing and Network Theory as part of the SONET program. It has been at the forefront in organizing institutional training courses for scientists of various R&D institutions and national level associations.

Very recently, the department has been equipped with a Microwave Lab in association with "ASTRA Microwave Products", a Multimedia Lab in association with "Analog Devices", a VLSI and an Embedded Systems Lab under "TEQIP".

Achievements

  • SSS PROJECT

    The Sustainability Support Scheme (SSS) Programe of the Ministry of Information Technology, Government of India, took part. The main objectives of this Project were to make the department self-sustainable through earning by way of conducting (Continuing Engineering Education Programe) CEEP courses, consultancy etc...

  • TEQUIP Project

    Under Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme (TEQUIP) many new Laboratories like VLSI, Advanced Communication Systems, Digital Signal Processing Speech Signal Processing are established.

  • SONET Program

    The Department has also contributed learning modules in the SONET Program in the areas of Communication Engineering, DSP and Network Theory.
