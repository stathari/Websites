Prasthuthi





Paper Presentation

"Your ideas we boost, So you can rule the roost"

Calling all techies!!!! Think you've explored new waves in today's technology that others haven't? Enter Prasthuthi, the platform to "communicate" your ideas and share your cognizant views...

We present to you PRASTHUTHI, a paper presentation competition for all students possessing a penchant to delve into the multi-faceted engineering world. Here's the signal to unplug your reservoirs of information... and stay tuned for some exciting prizes! So put on your thinking caps and let the ideas flow!!!

Judging Criteria:
a.Originality of the idea
b.Presentation skills
c.Practical scope and extent of implementation
d.Team work

TOPICS:

1. Communications:
* Wireless sensor networks
* Mobile Communications
* Optical fibre
* Satellite Communication
* Navigation
2. VLSI Design

3. Embedded Systems

4. Microwave Technologies
* RF Circuits
* Radar Systems
5. Digital Signal Processing
* Image Processing
* Speech Processing
* Signal Processing

6. NANO Technology
7. Artificial Neural Networks and Fuzzy Logic
8. MEMS
9. Computer Networking & Network Security
10. Energy Harvesting, Sustainability, Smart Grid

Rules:

• Each team can have at most 3 members, belonging to the same or different colleges.

• The papers have to be in the IEEE format not exceeding 6 pages.

A sample format can be downloaded here*

* Strictly the paper should be in IEEE format, else will be rejected.

• The paper and any part of it must not be plagiarized from “white” papers.

• Soft copy of the paper must be mailed to aakruthi2k13@gmail.com by February 20th, 2013, 11:59 P.M. The subject should be “Prasthuthi- Topic Name”.

• The selected teams have to pay a participation fee of Rs. 300/- per head in form of DD in favour of "Head, Dept. of E.C.E, E.C.E Association Account" to be posted to the Department address (Participation fee includes lunch for two days) along with two hard copies of your presentation &; photocopies of your ID cards .

• Accommodation fee for each person is Rs. 500/- for two days of the event.
(Accommodation fee includes breakfast and dinner for two days).

• On the event day, all the participants ‘must’ have ID cards of their respective colleges (else they will not be allowed to take part in the event).

• The participating team would be given 10 minutes to present their paper (in which all participants have to speak) and 2 minutes are allotted for queries.

• Judges decision will be final and further queries on the matter shall not be entertained.

Register your team for acknowledgement.
