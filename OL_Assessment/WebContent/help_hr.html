Employee Performance Management

Review Manager:

Login with your login Credentials provided to you. Once logged into the HR Dashboard, there are 3 choices,

  • Competency
  • Assessment Status
  • Report Generation

On clicking competency, the competencies of either of the 5 deparments are to be edited. The questions to be asked are also editable under this tab.

Here, department2 was selected and the Questions are being edited.
Now, in the "Assessment Status", select any one of the Departments.
Here, every employee is shown along with his assessment status. Moving the cursor over the Employee shows the scores.



On clicking the "Report Generation" , Reports can be generated either based upon Position, Department. Or even the competency report can be generated depending on the Department.
Now, on clicking any one of those, the report is generated which can be Downloaded or Printed.
